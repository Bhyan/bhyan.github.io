About Me

My name is Bryan Brito, I was born in São Paulo, Brasil, although I live in Natal, Rio Grande do Norte more than 20 years. I have a Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology for Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Norte. I currently work as a Quality Engineer, focused on Test Automation with several tools.

I’m interested in several Software Engineering topics, including: Quality, Design Patterns, Information Security, Architecture, DevOps, etc. I really enjoy learning and testing new tools and languages.

Recently I started to be more involved in software development and open source communities, meetups and conferences. And of course, I have contributed to some projects. In my free time I usually read books, learn about coffee, watch series (Star Trek) and movies.

I hope you can get something valuable out of the content I share here. If you want to get in touch, leave a comment or look for me on Linkedin. You can also check out my projects on Github, and don’t forget to subscribe to this blog’s RSS Feed.
